Admission Requirements

Qualifications

  • The students must be a high school graduate. However, if they were not able to take the Alternative Learning System (ALS) and pass the Philippine Educational Placement Test to qualify for college
  • The students must pass the College Admission Test of the University
  • Must be of Good Moral Character or Transferee from other colleges/universities

Requirements and Fees

  • Form 138- High School Report Card (Orginal and Photocopy)
  • Recommendation Letter from High school principal and guidance councilor
  • Certificate of Good Moral
  • Copy of High School Diploma
  • Copy of NSO Certified birth certificate
  • Most recent medical and dental health record
  • Latest 2x2 ID Picture

For Foreign Students

  • Pass the PSU-CAT and PSU English Proficiency Test prior to admission
  • Submit the following:
    • Valid Student VISA
    • Original Trnascript of Records (OTR) with English Translation
    • Identification card issued by the Philippine Bureu of Immigration TOEFL or IELTS
New College Freshmen Students

Students shall be accepted regardless of age, sex, nationality, religious beliefs, socio-economic status, or political affiliations.

Students must have graduated from recognized secondary schools.

Students must qualify in the average grade requirement (GWA) set by PSU or by CHED. Must qualify in the interview

Students enrolled in vocational courses or those not leading to a degree program are admitted as beginning freshmen.

Transferees

Must have complete and valid credentials.

Must meet all the prescribed admission requirements of the University and the concerned College.

Qualifying Test for Transferees.

Average grade requirement (GWA).

Copy of grades signed by the Registrar or OTR for reference purposes.

Certificate of Transfer Credential.

Interview.

Physical or Health Examination.

Certificate of Good Moral Character.

Copy of NSO birth Certificate.

Must not have any outstanding failure in any academic subjects.

Must not have any outstanding failure in any non-academic subject (including NSTP).

Only students who have enrolled in any course leading to a degree program are admitted as transferees.

Admission shall be on a probation basis during the first semester of stay in the University or until such time as they shall have validated/repeated in accordance with the University policies all the subjects taken outside the PSU which are required for their course.

Official Transcript of Record (OTR) shall be submitted prior to admission for the following semester; otherwise, enrollment will not be allowed.

ADMISSION PROCEDURES

  • Get/secure application form from the:
    1. Guidance Office/Student Services Office
    2. Admission and Testing Office
  • Properly accomplish PSU-CAT application form and attach recent two(2) identical 2X2 photo with name tag
  • Submit accomplished PSU-CAT form with requirements and the official receipt to the Guidance Office/Student Services Office
  • Get examination test permit

DURING THE EXAMINATION

  1. Go to designated testing center specified in the examination permit
  2. Bring Mongol pencil #2
  3. Present examination permit to the examiner

Results will be pulished thru internet at the official psu website
