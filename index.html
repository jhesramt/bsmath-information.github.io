About the Program

The BS Mathematics program is a rigorous and comprehensive four-year degree course that offers a deep dive into the world of mathematics. Through this program, students develop a solid foundation in mathematical theory and applications, which are essential for addressing a wide range of real-world challenges

Throughout the four-year journey, students explore core mathematical disciplines, such as algebra, calculus, statistics, and more, honing their analytical and problem-solving skills. This program is designed to provide students with a well-rounded education in mathematics while also offering the flexibility to customize their studies to align with their unique interests and career goals
